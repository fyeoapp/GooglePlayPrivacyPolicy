FYCEO Mobile Privacy Policy

What personal information is collected?

The FYCEO Mobile app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information with anyone or anything.

What other types of information may we collect?

Bug report information

When you send us a bug report, we may collect anonymous information about your device.
The information sent about your device does not contain any personally identifiable information.
The information is not sent to any third parties and only kept for our records.

The information sent is strictly the following:
OS Version (example: "API: 19 (KitKat)" for Android, "iOS 7" for iOS)
Build (example: "4.4.3" for Android, "7.1.0" for iOS)
Brand (example: "LG")
Model (example: "Nexus 4")
Internet Connectivity (example "true")

That information that is collected is strictly used to help identify issues, such as an issue specific to a brand.

We may, at our discretion, save issue statistics. This information may be used for diagnosing potential issues with specific brands before they appear in the retail application.

You have the option to opt out and not send any device information by not checking to send anonymous information. We respect this, and will not send any device information.

You also may request that we may contact you in regards to the bug report. Your e-mail address is strictly used to contact you regarding any issues, and that information will be discarded after the issue has been resolved. We do not share your e-mail with any third parties.

Android Permissions

[Access Network State]

[Internet]

To access the latest announcements, tweets, and gallery pictures, we require an internet connection.

[Read External Storage]

[Write External Storage]

We save information/pictures for offline mode, and this requires us to read and write to a storage device.

What if I have a question or complaint?

If you have any questions or wish to file a complaint, please feel free to e-mail us at fyceo.app@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time, as we reserve the right to change it, so please check back periodically to ensure that you are aware of any changes. If we make a material change to this Privacy Policy, we will note this on the application description.
